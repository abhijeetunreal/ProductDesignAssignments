Screen-Based Apps:
Mindfulness apps (e.g., Calm, Headspace) require screen engagement, perpetuating distraction. 60% of users abandon them due to notification fatigue (Statista).

Passive Wearables:
Fitness trackers (e.g., Fitbit, Apple Watch) prioritize physical health; mindfulness features are shallow (e.g., generic breathing reminders).

Analog Tools:
Journals or timers lack interactivity and fail to create real-time, sensory connections to the present.
